What is this based on?

This was based on the price of the cherry in game, which is 100 Bells. Given the size of the cherrys in game and the average price for a pound of cherries being $3.08 I decided lets make a single cherry item about a third of a pound or $1.027. If this Dollar amount is worth 100 bells then 1 bell is about $0.1027. This is close to the conversion rate of the Yen and makes in game prices close-ish to real world prices.
